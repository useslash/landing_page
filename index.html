How it works

It's highly customizable

It's not a no-code tool, Slash is low-code. We provide all the infrastructure that your team needs to integrate with any API and to create data pipelines. You can add validations, map, filter, and format the data in the way that your application need. We give your team freedom to think only in the business rules, we take care of the infrastructure.

It's reliable

It's the reliable infrastructure that you need to launch your product. We guarantee that once we receive your data, it will be saved and you can re-run your pipeline whenever you need it. It's super easy to debug, find problems, and fix your data. Also, you have full transparency about errors in your pipeline, so you can fix and re-run.

It's collaborative

It's easy to stimulate collaboration between domain experts and tech teams. Let the teams work together, find better solutions, and create awesome data pipelines.
